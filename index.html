Our Story

A Little Café with
a Big Heart

Founded in 2018, Brew & Bloom started as a small corner café in Banjara Hills. We believe great coffee is more than a drink — it is a moment of calm in your busy day.

Every bean is sourced directly from farms in Coorg and Araku Valley. Our bakers arrive at 5am every morning to make everything fresh from scratch, because you deserve nothing less.
